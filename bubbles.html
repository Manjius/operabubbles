Opera & Bubbles

A luminous evening where classical aria meets playful cloudbursts of bubbles. A chamber ensemble, shimmering lights, and a moving story about breath, joy, and impossible notes floating into the rafters.

About the performance

In Opera & Bubbles, the city of Bellacqua is preparing for its annual moonlit concert when the lead soprano loses her confidence after a silent rehearsal. Instead of retreating, she follows a mysterious bubble-maker through backstage tunnels, discovering that every breath can become music.

The production blends warm chamber strings, percussive water effects, and projected constellations. Bubbles rise from the orchestra pit, drifting above the audience like tiny spotlights. Some carry fragments of melody, others burst into glittering harmonies timed to the chorus.

Expect humor, tenderness, and a finale that turns the whole theatre into a singing sky. The language is English with Dutch and German surtitles.
